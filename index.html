How To Play

A game for two players (or teams). Player 1 secretly picks a face. Player 2 asks yes/no questions to figure out who it is. But unlike the popular kids' game, questions must be based on what vibe each person gives off.

Think: "Has your person ever been to a rave?" or "Does your person host their own podcast?" or "Does your person leave voice notes?"

Click any face to close it off as you eliminate people.

The aim is to guess the person Player 1 chose in as few questions as possible, but really, the aim is to laugh at stereotypes and AI-generated faces.
