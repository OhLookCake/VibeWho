# VibeWho

A game for two players. Player 1 secretly picks a face. Player 2 asks yes/no questions to figure out who it is. But unlike the popular kids' game, questions must be based on what vibe each person gives off.  

Think: 
* "Has your person ever been to a rave?"  
* "Does your person host their own podcast?"  
* "Does your person send voice notes?"  

Click any face to close it off as you eliminate people. The aim is to guess the person Player 1 chose in as few questions as possible, but really, the aim is to laugh at stereotypes and AI-generated faces.
